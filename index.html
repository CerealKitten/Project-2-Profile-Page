CerealKitten Stickers

A candid selfie of myself shortly before getting ready for class one morning followed by some stickers I designed. I believe i was fully clothed beneath my neck in this picture, though I don't see how that is relevant so why did you even ask?

This series of stickers was meant to be an introduction to a company/business I am trying to start for my own personal pursual. The border of the stickers was meant to act as a window pane. This would make the cat head outline (my logo and therefore my work) a window into everything. This is further explored through the photography contained within each iteration of the logo. Each of these pictures was taken during a normal day in the life of me. This means, in a sense, that these are not only windows into the world around us, but also into my life.

Each of these stickers is printed on waterproof vinyl can be stuck to anything. Each different design also has a name that I thought sounded like a cat name. I thought this would be a cute way to introduce my business's brand and it was just fun to make these, so I wanted the fun to be reflected in the naming of the stickers as well.
